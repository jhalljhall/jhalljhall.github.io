### The Coal Prince

### A Dark Holler Comics Production

**Dark Holler Comics** is a new entity from the mind of Justin Hall.  As writer, artist, and producer, Justin Hall sees DHC as a way to build worlds, tell stories, and share new experiences through a range of digital platforms.

The first offering from DHC will be a story about Appalachia's first super hero.

There are no "Appalachian super heroes" in pop culture and comicdom today.  There might be a few geographically linked, but there are no stories, no real hero that can be identified as Appalachia.
{:.lead}

### Introduction

**The Coal Prince** is a story about a young boy from a coal camp in the heart of Appalachia that becomes a hero.
{:.lead}

### Premise

_Stone_, a young boy that discovers he has powers to manipulate time and see thru almost anything is able to save his father and 41 other coal miners from a mining disaster.  After learning of his powers and his struggle to use them, Stone is quickly thrust in the middle of a geopolitical war in the homeland he loves.  He uses his abilities to weigh heavily on the outcome and learns a lesson on control, power, and choosing to live a simple life.  He uses the lessons learned to help the people he loves who are often in the middle of a feud, wrongdoing, or injustice.
