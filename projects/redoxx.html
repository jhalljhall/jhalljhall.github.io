ReDoxx

Project Info:

ReDoxx is a Deck Building card game where multiple players face-off to be the last hacker standing. Players compete against each other to gather as many ReDoxx Coins as they can by completing hacks. Hacks are completed when specific targets are aquired through computer hacking. ReDoxxCoins are provided for the aquired targets which are sold on the Black Market.
