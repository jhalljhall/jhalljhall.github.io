Middle Tech Podcast

Project Info:

Middle Tech was created to advance the business ecosystem in the Midwest by providing news, entertainment, and educational content. I got the idea to do a developer series focused around trends and pain points local developers might find helpful. After talking with the gang at MiddleTech we were able to organize and launch the Developer Deep Dive Series. It provides a voice that follows along with their mission statement and so far we've had great reviews and discussions that have spawned from the episodes.
