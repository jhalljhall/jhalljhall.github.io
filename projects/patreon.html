Project Info:

Dark Holler Comics® is a publishing platform. Dark Holler Comics® is providing a peek into the creative process and first glances of the concept sketches and finished pieces being released only on Patreon. Join our Patreon to support our storytellers.

  1. Subscribe to the Dark Holler Comic®'s podcast
  2. Listen to audible versions of the episodes and stories!
  3. Subscribe to the Patreon page
  4. Watch as stories from the podcast are brought to life!
  5. Receive concept sketches and notes from the storyteller's sketchbooks and notebooks
  6. Provide feedback to Dark Holler Comics® as we tell new old and new stories in a unique way. Let's go through this unique journey together!
