Baseball For Everyone

Project Info:

Baseball For Everyone is a Deck Building card game. Make your own player, collect other players, build a team, and play the game of baseball. BFE is a deck building game that replicates the feel of baseball. Players try to out smart each other by manipulating 'the count'.

Experience the strategy of baseball on a level that other games have yet to deliver. Players will compete in a head-to-head matchup. Choose a strategy with a custom deck comprising of a manager, players, and action cards. The team with the most runs at the end of the game wins!
