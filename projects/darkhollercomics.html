Dark Holler Comics®

Project Info:

Dark Holler Comics® is a publishing platform. Normal comic book conventions, such as a book, don't translate well on common devices like phones. In order to target the most commonly used devices the comic book format has to change. Dark Holler Comics provides a solution for publishing media such as graphic novels on common devices for a standard streamlined experience for the reader.

Project Details:

  • Industry:Comics and Graphic Novels
  • Tech Stack(s):Flutter (iOS, Android), Firebase, Drupal
