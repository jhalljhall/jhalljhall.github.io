MTG: Re-imagined

Project Info:

MTG: Re-imagined is starting out as two different projects.

The first project will result in an ongoing gallery of Magic: The Gathering Cards previously released where the artist re-imagines the card art for each card.

The second project will result in a website that will allow you to make your own sets with your own play styles, cards, mechanics, and card art from previously released Magic: The Gathering cards. The sets you create will be available for purchase.
